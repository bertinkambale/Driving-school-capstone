PAUL-DRIVING-SCHOOL
(Kinshasa(DRC))

PAUL-DRIVING-SCHOOL is driving-school for all the people interested and passionate in becoming drivers
PAUL-DRIVING-SCHOOL created in 2022 by Mr Kambale kamulete bertin who is himself a Congolese who found that it is nessesary to know how to drive in our daily life because of language barriers.
That's why PAUL-DRIVING-SCHOOL is going to help people to increase their capacity of driving, to avoid accidante in the road , there many lessons of driving that is going to help people to be mature and to know each code of conduct of driving
With all flexible programs, you can choose a program that meets your expectations!
We have more than 30 certified teachers and tutors; with a lot of experience. We hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a strong community of learners.
